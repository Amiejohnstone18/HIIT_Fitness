Meet Our Personal Trainers

All of our Personal Trainers at HIIT Fitness have extensive knowledge and experience in the industry. Each PT specialises in a specific area to ensure our customers are always properly informed whether that be in classes or personal training sessions to esnure you are getting the best out of your workout.

To book in a personal training session with one of our Trainers, email hiit-fitness@gmail.com or enquire with the front desk in the gym.

MAX

With 10 years' worth of experience under his belt and a master's degree in strength and conditioning training, Max is the go-to guy on how to get bigger and stronger. Competing in several IFBB competitions, he is a highly respected trainer in his field for someone so young.

LAUREN

Boasting an extremely all-round sport background, Lauren has represented team GB in netball gaining a huge knowledge on how an athlete needs to function day in day out. She has been in the industry for 3 years now and is our newest member of the team.

PAUL

For the Cross Fit fanatics Paul is your man. Still competing all year round and fully focused on training. Normally found working out with his clients this guy never stops. If you really want to dial in on fine tuning your goals and working with an active athlete, make sure you get hold of Paul.

GABBY

Our yoga and flexibility specialist with almost every qualification under the sun in yoga/Pilates and flexibility. Gaby has done further studies in the link between mindset and performance. If your looking to get that mindful edge or rehabilitating from an injury be sure to pick up a tip or two off her.

BRAD

Calisthenics and gymnastics are in Brad's blood. Having been a gymnast from a young age brad moved into personal training 8 years ago and is our longest serving personal trainer of 5 years. You'll be sure to catch him in our newly fitted gymnastics area. Him and Gaby work closely together on classes and rehab.

JOHN

The gym manager John has been in the industry for 13 years and is our most experienced personal trainer. He's worked with Chelsea football club as well as many A- list celebrities. Nowadays more than often with a clip board in his hand or in the office, John still offers a small range personal training slot's.
