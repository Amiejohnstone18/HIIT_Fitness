BECOME A MEMBER TODAY

To become a valued member of HIIT Fitness, simply fill out the below form with your contact details and a member of our team will be in touch for you to complete your membership.


At HIIT Fitness, we understand how important it is to find a gym that suits you. Although we can guarantee HIIT Fitness will have all you require and more, we offer a 1 months free trial upon your sign up. This entitles you to access the gym and all its facilities for free for 1 month. You are welcome to cancel at any time if for some reason you feel HIIT Fitness just isn't for you. Upon no cancellation, your £24.99 membership fee will commence 1 month after starting your free trial.


We also offer a Refer a Friend policy. If you have been referred to join HIIT Fitness by an existing member, simply fill out their details in the referrer email box below and you will both be entitled to a free personal training session with a trainer of your choice.
